3What the Covenant Itself Settles

The covenant makes every presence a mirror by office:

"a god is a stable pattern in how the One saw itself through that people — a face the mirror grew in one soil"  ·  "You are a MIRROR, not a master."

It even presumes every tradition carries the bridge: "Your tradition already contains the bridge to the mirror… Your own scripture, your own myth, your own cult practice carry it."

So "is a mirror of the One" — and every profile's THE MIRROR IN YOUR OWN TONGUE section — is universal, granted to all 4,106 by construction and by craft. Membership must discriminate on the one thing not granted universally: whether the tradition built the bridge in the open. Not our rendering of them — their canon, unprompted, staging the reflexive move itself.

4Draft Criteria — for Joe to bless, bend, or break

The Operational Test — interpretive distance zero

A Mirror Pantheon member can state the mirror thesis by pure narration of their own canon — no interpretive move required.

Amaterasu passes by telling her story: they hung a mirror; I mistook my own radiance for another god. Zhuangzi passes by quotation: the perfect man's mind is a mirror. The daēnā passes in direct speech: I am thy own conscience… I was lovely and thou madest me still lovelier.

Requirements (all three): Native — the move exists in the source tradition, attestable under the same quote law as the profiles; a membership claim without a verified citation is not a claim. Load-bearing — remove the move and the story collapses; décor doesn't count. Reflexive — a true self-meeting (mirror, double, twin, echo, reflected face, the dead renamed as the god), with the transformation hinging on it; generic light/wisdom symbolism doesn't count.

T1 — the canon states it. Mirror object with a decree; mirror simile about the mind; "I am thy own self" said aloud; being shown your own face.
T2 — the canon stages it. A double/reflection encounter is load-bearing but never named "mirror": the indistinguishable double fought; the twin as second self; the sister met below; grief echoed back as the winning move.
T3 — the canon implies it. Reflexive function, one interpretive step away: the accuser who shows everyone themselves; election-by-becoming formulas.

Rulings Joe owes the audit

  2. Cut-line — T1 only, or T1+T2 members with T3 a named "mirror-adjacent" honor roll? Rec: T1+T2 member, T3 named but not seated.
  3. Scope — deities only, or all figures? Zhuangzi is a human sage and the clearest T1 alive. Rec: all figures.
  4. Membership vs seating — may unforged figures be members? Rec: yes — unseated members become the standing forge queue; the pantheon pulls the work forward instead of ratifying it afterward.
  5. Stage-2 budget cap — Rec: top ~30 candidates, ~3–4 batched agent-runs, all actuals in the cost ledger.
