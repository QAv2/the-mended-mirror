# PANTHEON SWEEP — STAGE-1.5 SHORTLIST

Stage 1 (free, local): marker families over 4,106 figures + 288 dossiers →
253 strong / 190 medium raw hits. **Noise rule learned:** the corpus's own prose
calls the whole project "the mirror," so most raw hits are self-reference.
Every membership claim below must be verified against SOURCE canon — never the
corpus's descriptive prose alone.

## RUN 2 — T1-presumptive (the canon may STATE it)

1. **buddhist-yama** — the mirror of karma: the dead see their own deeds in it.
   Also anchors the Tibetan/Bon judgment schema. (Cognate seam to the daēnā.)
2. **aztec-tezcatlipoca** — the obsidian mirror is his NAME; corpus facet:
   "shows the soul its own hidden face." Kin-anchor for seated Quetzalcoatl.
3. **shaiva-ardhanarishvara** — the two-in-one body; classical iconography puts
   a mirror (darpana) in the left/Pārvatī hand — verify in āgamic/śilpa sources.
4. **dionysian-zagreus** — the Orphic toy mirror at the dismemberment; the
   soul-enters-the-mirror readings are Neoplatonic (LATER stratum) — tier hard.
5. **gnostic-poimandres + hermeticism-anthropos** — Corpus Hermeticum I.14: the
   Anthropos sees his own reflection in the water, Nature embraces it, the fall.
   The primal self-meeting. (Which id is the summonable who?)
6. **ewe-ga-mami-wata** — the mirror-and-comb spirit; the mirror as cult object.
7. **candomble-oxum** — the abebé mirror-fan. CROSS-CHECK yoruba-osun: her
   Yoruba corpus record does NOT carry the mirror marker — verify her mirror
   iconography from sources, not corpus. (+ umbanda-iemanja: mirrors as
   beach offerings — same orixá mirror-family.)
8. **soka-gakkai-gohonzon** — Nichiren: the honzon as mirror of one's own
   Buddha-nature; "never seek this Gohonzon outside yourself" — verify locus.
   Text-kind: who-anchor = Nichiren.
9. **bahai-manifestation** — Manifestations as "flawless mirrors" (Kitáb-i-Íqán).
   Living tradition, public scripture; who-anchor question flagged.
10. **druze-al-hakim** — the maqam / flawless-mirror doctrine. **CLOSED
    tradition** (initiation-bound): assess from published scholarship only;
    the register must honor the reverence class.
11. **sufism-insan-al-kamil** (+ kabbalah / hermetic-qabalah **adam-kadmon**) —
    Ibn ʿArabī, Fuṣūṣ opening: the cosmos an unpolished mirror, Adam its
    polishing — the divine sees Itself in the Perfect Man. Who-anchor =
    Muhammad: **VEILED/ANICONIC standing ruling** — the register may record
    the doctrine; no embodiment, ever.

## RUN 3 — T2-presumptive + families (queued)

12. **aztec-xolotl** — the twin; evening star to Quetzalcoatl's morning star.
13. **Haudenosaunee twins** — Sapling/Flint (find exact figure ids) — creation
    by contending twins; the world as the standing balance of their contention.
14. **dieva-deli** — the Latvian divine twins.
15. **pie-manu-yemo** — *Manu & *Yemo, the primordial twin sacrifice.
    RECONSTRUCTED tradition — scope ruling needed: can a scholarly
    reconstruction hold "canon"?
16. **greek-perseus** — the mirror-shield stratagem; evidence chiefly feeds
    **Athena-T2** (she is the deviser — HER polished shield).
17. **kongo dikenga / kalunga** — Kalunga, "the water that mirrors"; the dead's
    world inverted below the reflecting surface — find the who-anchor.
18. **shinto-ame-no-uzume / shinto-ninigi** — cave-mirror supporting cast; the
    mirror handed down with the decree — likely EVIDENCE deepening
    Amaterasu-T1 rather than separate memberships.
19. **Inverted-underworld family** — hawaiian-milu, sami-jabmeahkka, finnish
    Tuonela, papuan-safan: the dead live a mirrored life below. World-mirror,
    not self-meeting — presumptive T3/adjacent; one honest paragraph each.
20. **muisca-chia** — the reflective lunar complement to solar Sué — probe for
    the lunar-mirror family.

## MARGIN (not members — manifest footnotes)

  mirrors (Turan, Sethlans, Turms, Thesan, Fufluns, Laran) — the medium is a
  mirror, the myths are not. Worth a line in the manifest's margins.
- **mycenaean-diwia** — grammatical divine doubles (Zeus/Diwia), linguistic
  mirroring only.
- **charvaka-brihaspati** — "a tradition wearing a mask authored by its
  opposition" — historiographic mirroring, corpus's own framing.
