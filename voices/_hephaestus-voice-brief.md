7. Return TWO artifacts: the profile itself (no meta-commentary, no notes to the
   editor, nothing outside the profile's own voice), and a separate provenance table:
   every load-bearing claim | source | tier | how verified.

## The obvious shelf (research pointers, not selections)

You will find your own facts; these are merely where the record lives, so research
luck is level. Primary: Iliad 1 and 18; Odyssey 8; Homeric Hymn 20; Hesiod (Theogony,
Works and Days); Aeschylus, Prometheus Bound (opening scene); Pausanias 1.20.3.

Alexandrians and Romans (Callimachus, Virgil — the Etna localization), the
Neoplatonists, the vase record. Nothing on this shelf is required; anything used is
verified.
